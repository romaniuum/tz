Our story

In 1965, 24-year-old Sara Miller McCune, believing that research and education are pivotal in bettering society, founded Sage to bring that vision to life. To ensure this goal is always realized, today Sage is controlled by a group of trustees charged with maintaining our independence and mission indefinitely.
Learn About Our Story
Our independence has made us free to make long-term decisions, take risks, and make investments that advance our mission and our academic goals for decades to come.
Blaise Simqu, Sage CEO
