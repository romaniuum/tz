About us

Micro Focus

In 2023, OpenText™ acquired Micro Focus™, a global software leader in enterprise digital transformation since 1976.

Micro Focus products now enhance the OpenText Analytics Cloud, OpenText DevOps Cloud and OpenText Cybersecurity Cloud. Additionally, the Micro Focus products have broadened our offerings to include OpenText IT Operations Cloud and Portfolio solutions.

To learn more about our products, see Products A-Z listing

For a list of recently rebranded product names, refer to Product Name Changes.

OpenText Partner Program for Micro Focus products

OpenText welcomes Micro Focus partners into our enriched ecosystem. You can still visit the Partner Portal for support and assistance on former Micro Focus products, or find out more about OpenText products on our website.
