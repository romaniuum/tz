15 JANUARY 2025

Cyprus signed the Fourth Additional Protocol to the European Convention on Extradition (CETS No. 212).

10 JANUARY 2025

Rwanda acceded to the Convention on Cybercrime (ETS No. 185) and to the Additional Protocol to the Convention on cybercrime, concerning the criminalisation of acts of a racist and xenophobic nature committed through computer systems (ETS No. 189).

7 JANUARY 2025

The Philippines ratified the Convention on Mutual Administrative Assistance in Tax Matters as amended by the 2010 Protocol (ETS No. 127).

20 december 2024

Romania ratified the Additional Protocol to the European Charter of Local Self-Government on the right to participate in the affairs of a local authority (CETS No. 207).
