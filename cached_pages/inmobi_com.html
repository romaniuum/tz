About InMobi

Founded in 2007, mobile is the core of InMobi. We have grown InMobi Advertising to one of the largest platforms for advertisers and publishers to reach their customers on mobile devices. In 2019, InMobi built Glance, a platform that is helping consumers around the globe discover everything they love on their lock screen and other surfaces. Tomorrow, we see a convergence of AI, Advertising, Entertainment, and Shopping to create personal mobile experiences on a mass scale, ushering in the Surface Economy.
