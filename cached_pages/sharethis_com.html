When Oracle Steps Down, ShareThis Steps Up

With Oracle’s exit from the advertising business, ShareThis is committed to ensuring a seamless transition for all of our partners and clients. 

ShareThis offers powerful solutions for both behavioral and contextual data targeting, delivering the precision and effectiveness your campaigns need to succeed.

Did you know?

With over 1,500 standard segments and custom segment capabilities, there is a ShareThis audience segment ready to fill any gap left by Oracle’s deprecation. And, our audiences are available across all leading DSPs for easy activation. 

We also provide the flexibility to deploy our data wherever you need it.
