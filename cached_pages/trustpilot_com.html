By October 2024, having consumed only one class and with no resolution for our refund, we realized that Planet Spark is a cleverly set up scam service. They pretend to be a legitimate business, providing a few initial classes, but eventually stop responding or scheduling classes, hoping you forget about the money you paid. After getting frustrated with this back and forth, I wrote my initial review in October 2024 on Trustpilot, and then I received communication from Planet Spark that they would provide a refund if I took down my review. Hoping they would refund my money, I took down my review, but as of January 2025, I have not received any refund. I urge potential customers to be cautious and thoroughly research before committing to their services. To summarise at the end, I think these guys are a bunch of scammers who have set up nice legitimate-looking fronts to SCAM people.
