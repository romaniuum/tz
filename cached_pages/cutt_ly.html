FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a URL shortener and how does it work?

    A URL shortener creates a compact version of a long URL, often referred to as a tiny URL, making it short and easier to share. Cuttly offers an advanced link shortener that not only simplifies your links but also tracks analytics, allowing you to see how your short links perform.

  • What is the difference between a branded short link and a regular short link?

    A branded short link uses your custom domain, such as yourbrand.link/alias, instead of a generic domain. This helps build trust and increase click-through rates, while a regular short link uses a default domain like cutt.ly/url-shortener

  • How can I use QR codes with Cuttly?

    Cuttly allows you to generate customized QR codes for each short link, link in bio, or survey. You can adjust the design by adding colors, shapes, and even your logo to match your brand’s identity. QR codes are scannable and often used in marketing, product packaging, and advertisements to provide easy access to online content.

  • Can I create a link in bio page with Cuttly?

    Yes, Cuttly offers customizable link in bio pages. You can build a microsite with important links and track clicks to gain insights on engagement from social media platforms.

  • How does Cuttly help me track the performance of my short links?

    Cuttly provides detailed analytics for each short link, allowing you to track metrics such as total clicks, devices used, and geographic location. This helps you better understand your audience and optimize your campaigns.

  • Can I use Cuttly to create surveys?

    Yes, with Cuttly Surveys, you can create interactive surveys using various question types like multiple-choice or ratings. These surveys are easy to share using short links or QR codes, and you can analyze responses to improve your business strategies.

  • Why is Cuttly ideal for TRAI-compliant SMS marketing in India?

    Cuttly is TRAI-compliant, meaning you can use our short links with custom headers for SMS marketing campaigns in India, adhering to local telecom regulations.

  • How long do short links created with Cuttly last?

    Links created with Cuttly do not expire by default, and you can manage or delete them at any time from your dashboard.

  • Can I use my own custom domain for URL shortening with Cuttly?

    Yes, Cuttly supports custom domains, allowing you to create branded short links that match your domain, boosting trust and brand consistency.

  • What makes Cuttly different from other URL shorteners?

    Cuttly offers a comprehensive Link Management Platform with advanced features like branded short links, detailed analytics, QR code generation, link in bio pages, and interactive surveys. It’s more than just a short link tool—Cuttly is a full link management solution.
