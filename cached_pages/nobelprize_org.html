Nobel Prizes 2024

  • Physics

    They used physics to find patterns in information

    This years laureates used tools from physics to construct methods that helped lay the foundation for todays powerful machine learning.

  • Chemistry

    They cracked the code for proteins’ amazing structures

    The Nobel Prize in Chemistry 2024 is about proteins, lifes ingenious chemical tools.

  • Physiology or medicine

    Tiny RNAs with profound physiological importance

    Victor Ambros and Gary Ruvkun discovered microRNA, a new class of tiny RNA molecules that play a crucial role in gene regulation.

  • Literature

    Han Kang

    The Nobel Prize in Literature 2024 is awarded to the South Korean author Han Kang, for her intense poetic prose that confronts historical traumas and exposes the fragility of human life.

  • Peace

    A powerful international norm stigmatising nuclear weapons

    The grassroots movement of atomic bomb survivors from Hiroshima and Nagasaki, also known as Hibakusha, is receiving the Nobel Peace Prize for its efforts to achieve a world free of nuclear weapons and for demonstrating through witness testimony that nuclear weapons must never be used again.

  • Economic sciences

    An explanation for why some countries are rich and others poor

    This years laureates have provided new insights into why there are such vast differences in prosperity between nations.

Nobel Prize Concert 2024

A magnificent musical celebration of science

Swedish soprano Malin Bystrm and conductor Petr Popelka performed together with the Royal Stockholm Philharmonic Orchestra at the Nobel Prize Concert in Konserthuset Stockholm on 8 December. They performed a programme of dance-inspired music by Vtezslava Kaprlov, Richard Strauss and Sergei Rachmaninoff.

In memoriam

Sir J. Fraser Stoddart 19422024

Sir J. Fraser Stoddart passed away on 30 December 2024, aged 82. He was awarded the Nobel Prize in Chemistry 2016 for the design and synthesis of molecular machines.

Biography

Jimmy Carter passes away aged 100

Former US President Jimmy Carter passed away on 29 December 2024. He was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize 2002 for his decades of untiring effort to find peaceful solutions to international conflicts, to advance democracy and human rights, and to promote economic and social development.

Biography

Martin Karplus dies aged 94

Chemist Martin Karplus passed away on 28 December 2024, aged 94. He was awarded the 2013 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for the development of multiscale models for complex chemical systems.

Biography

Leon N. Cooper passes away

Physicist Leon N. Cooper passed away on 23 October 2024. He was awarded the 1972 Nobel Prize in Physics for their jointly developed theory of superconductivity, usually called the BCS-theory.
