Notice on Reduced Support for Wear OS Content for Users with Non-Samsung Devices in China 2025-01-15

Hello! This is the Galaxy Store Seller Portal team. Thank you for using Seller Portal and for your continued interest. Until now, for users in China, even when using a third-party smartphone and connected to a Galaxy Watch of Wear OS (including Watch4 and later models), users could access the Galaxy Store from their smartphones to download watch content. However, due to changes in the Galaxy Store's operating policy, we will be reducing services for third-party phones. The service change will proceed according to the procedure below: Finally, as of December 2025, downloading watch content through the Galaxy Store on third-party phones will no longer be possible. Even after that date, however, free contents can still be downloaded newly or existing contents can be re-downloaded by running the Galaxy Store installed inside the watch. ■ Schedules Schedule Contents March 31st, 2025 New downloads of paid content via third-party phones will cease. June 30th, 2025 New downloads of free content via third-party phones will stop. December 31st, 2025 Redownloads of previously purchased content via third-party phones will end. ■ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) Q1 If a user who purchased paid content requests a refund due to the discontinuation of sales, should a refund be provided? A1 Under the Galaxy Store Terms of Use, we have no obligation to provide refunds due to service interruption. Q2 How long can users who purchase paid content use that content? A1 Purchased content can continue to be used because it can be re-downloaded through the Galaxy Store inside the watch. If you have any questions, please visit Seller Portal > Help > Contact us for inquiries. Thank you, Galaxy Store Seller Portal Team

GSS User Acquisition Metrics Adjustment for 2024 2025-01-10

Hello, this is the Galaxy Store Seller Portal team. Thank you for using Seller Portal and for your continued interest. During the first quarter of 2025, we will be recovering missing user acquisition statistics in the Galaxy Store Statistics (GSS) system. The statistics in the user acquisition menu for 2024 may change while the data is being restored. ■ Target Menu - GSS > User Acquisition > Acquisition Reports > Channel, Source, Keywords ■ Date - January 13, 2025 ~ March 28, 2025 ■ Data Recovery Process - Execute sequential data recovery from most recent to oldest (December, 2024 → November, 2024 → ... → January, 2024) ■ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) Q1. In the User Acquisition menu, what statistics are provided? A1-1. Channel: Provides detailed page visit and download metrics for content according to users' channels entering Galaxy Store A1-2. Source: Provides detailed page visit and download metrics for content according to the source when users enter Galaxy Store through external apps. A1-3. Keywords: Provides detailed page visit and download metrics based on search keywords used by Galaxy Store users. Q2. Why can't I see these statistics from the User Acquisition menu? A2-1. The User Acquisition menu is provided for apps that have app detail page visits and downloads A2-2. Apps sold in China cannot provide statistics for user acquisition due to Chinese government policies that prevent data acquisition. A2-3. A selection of partners may not be able to view this user acquisition tab due to the regional restriction of the partners. Q3. In the User Acquisition submenu, why do statistics on the Featuring page differ from those in other submenus? A3. The Featuring page provides statistics on exhibition performance based on each service's main display area, applying different statistical calculation ranges from other menus. If you have any questions, please select Seller Portal > Help > Contact us. Thank you. Galaxy Store Seller Portal Team
